CASA SASA

an apartment in the heart of the Italian Alps

Instructions

Check In

Water

  • The main water valve is located right at the entrance, in the left cabinet behind the wooden door
  • Labeled with the name "Janíčková"

Electricity

  • Fuse box is located right after entering the building's main entrance
  • The main electricity switch is in the first cabinet on the left as you enter the apartment
  • Labeled with the name "Janíčková"

Gas & Boiler

  • Yellow gas valve next to/behind the pipe (half-open position means gas is flowing)
  • To turn on the boiler:
    • Second gas valve is located under the boiler (turn the valve handle halfway toward yourself to open)
    • Flip the wall switch next to the boiler to "Acceso" position
    • Turn on the boiler using the touch panel

Note: Clean bed linens are available in the cabinet. You can make up the beds and sofa bed in the bathroom. Down the stairs, there's a large closet with additional supplies (cola, extra linens, etc.).

Check Out

  • Please tidy up (vacuum, wipe surfaces if needed, clean the kitchen, etc.)
  • Lower the blinds and shutters
  • Empty and defrost the fridge and freezer
  • Turn off the boiler:
    • Switch off using the touch panel on the boiler, wait about 2 minutes, then flip the wall switch to OFF
  • Close the gas valves (both the pipe valve and boiler valve)
  • Turn off the electricity switch in the apartment AND the main switch in the hallway
  • Close the main water valve

Amenities & Notes

  • WiFi: No internet available. Mobile signal can be found on the balconies, though coverage varies
  • Entertainment: PS4 with NHL and Crash Bandicoot Racing games, also works as a DVD and Blu-ray player
  • Plumbing: Currently not working properly (fuses trip). Repair is in progress
  • Laundry: No washing machine available (coming soon)

Local Tips

  • Beer in Moggio: Visit Merlini bar and enjoy your drink with a view of Monte Pisimoni
  • Local cuisine: Try the regional specialty frico (fried cheese) and polenta prepared in various styles
  • Fresh bread & pastries: The bakery directly across from the apartment sells desserts from Illegio

Nearby Towns & Villages

  • Venzone - Beautifully preserved medieval walled town
  • Gemona - Hilltop town center worth exploring
  • Illegio - Charming mountain village
